This page is my favourite skateboarders.

It tells you about their life and why I like them.

One of my favourite skateboarders is Andy Schrock(left)
He is the owner of the companies
ReVive and Force wheels.He has a son called Ryden
and his wife is called Brittany.
He lives in Cincinnati, Ohio and works in
a place that he calls "The Shredquarters"(right)
I like him because he makes good videos and is good at skating
Another one of my favourite skateboarders is Sam Tabor(left)
He also lives in Cincinnati and is friends with Andy.
He makes videos and is very talented. He skates for
ReVive and works in The Shredquarters too.
I like him because he has a different style of skating
than most people as he likes No Comply tricks
and strange variations of lines.
