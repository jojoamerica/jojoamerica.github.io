This page is about skateboarding tricks.

It teaches you about some common tricks.

The first trick that most people learn is called the ollie.
The ollie is likely the most basic trick as
your feet never come off of the board.
The ollie is performed by pushing down
on the tail of the board with your
back foot(called popping) and sliding
your front foot up towards the nose of the board
This lifts the board of of the ground without
any spinning or flipping.
The kickflip is generally the next trick
that people learn.
It is similar to the ollie in the way
that there is no spin, but different
because there is flipping rotation. 		The kickflip is also performed by popping
the tail with your back foot and sliding your
other foot up towards the nose of the board
However this time the foot is slid to the side
when sliding your foot up and you flick out your ankle
The board should do a full rotation and
you land with both feet on the board.
